A Trip Around the World



A group of friends decided to travel the world. First, they went to Peru and met a Peruvian man named Carlos, who showed them Machu Picchu.

Then, they visited Greece, where a Greek woman named Maria told them stories about Greek gods.

Next, they flew to the USA. An American boy named Jack took them to see the Statue of Liberty. In the UK, a British girl named Emma showed them around London and Big Ben.

In Mexico, they met a Mexican girl named Sofia, who taught them to dance salsa and enjoy tacos. Then, they traveled to Japan, where a Japanese boy named Hiroshi showed them cherry blossoms and Mount Fuji.

After Japan, they visited China, where a Chinese girl named Mei took them to the Great Wall and shared dumplings. Finally, in France, they met a French boy named Pierre, who showed them the Eiffel Tower.

By the end, they had learned about different nationalities and made friends from all over the world!
