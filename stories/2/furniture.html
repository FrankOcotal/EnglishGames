Tommy's Furniture Adventure


Tommy is in a magic room. This is one bed. "This is fun!" says Tommy.

Next, he sees four chairs. "These are chairs," says Tommy.

Then, there are two tables. "These are tables," he says.

He sees seven sofas. "These are sofas," says Tommy.

Tommy finds three wardrobes. "These are wardrobes," says Tommy.

Finally, he sees five bookshelves. "These are bookshelves," he says.

Tommy counts, "One, two, three, four, five, six, seven." Tommy is happy with his new friends!
